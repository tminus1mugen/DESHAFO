What We Do

Our Programs

Five interconnected programs, one unified mission — transforming lives in Homabay County and beyond.

Program 01

Education & Mentorship

Our flagship educational program empowers the younger generation to identify their potential, understand academic pathways under the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), and build the skills needed for a successful future.

  • CBC curriculum guidance and academic support sessions
  • One-on-one and group mentorship for children and teens
  • Talent identification and development workshops
  • Career pathway mapping for secondary school students
  • DESHAFO Integrated School — accessible quality education
Program 02

Teen Mother Counselling & Reintegration

We believe no young woman's future should be defined by adversity. This program provides comprehensive support to teen mothers — helping them heal, grow, and return to school with confidence and community backing.

  • Psychosocial counselling and trauma-informed support
  • School reintegration advocacy and guidance
  • Life skills and parenting workshops
  • Community sensitization on SGBV awareness
  • Ongoing mentorship and peer support networks
Program 03 · Annual Event

The GRETECHI Conference

Held every August since 2015 in collaboration with MOSA Church, Homabay, the GRETECHI Conference is our annual flagship event — a gathering that brings together hundreds of children and teens for a transformative experience.

  • Spiritual formation and values-based programming
  • Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV) awareness sessions
  • Talent showcasing and performing arts
  • CBC pathway orientation and academic guidance
  • Reaches 300+ children and teens annually
  • Held in partnership with MOSA Church, Homabay
Program 04

Climate Action

DESHAFO recognizes that environmental sustainability is inseparable from community wellbeing. Our climate action program integrates green practices into daily community life, raising the next generation of environmental stewards.

  • Tree planting and environmental conservation drives
  • Environmental education in schools and community groups
  • Sustainable agriculture and food security workshops
  • Youth-led climate awareness campaigns
  • Integration of climate literacy into the GRETECHI Conference
Program 05

Community Reach & Leadership Development

Operating primarily across Homabay County, DESHAFO serves as a bridge — connecting underprivileged groups to resources, opportunities, and leadership training that builds stronger, more resilient communities.

  • Leadership workshops for youth and community members
  • Resource mobilization and referral services
  • Community health and welfare outreach
  • Partnerships with local churches, schools, and organizations
  • Media advocacy through Ramogi TV & Radio and Girwa Radio
