Founder & Executive Director

Rolex Ong'ute

MSc · Teacher · Mentor · Child Protection Advocate

A mother of three from Homabay County, Rolex Ong'ute is the heart and driving force behind DESHAFO Integrated CBO. Her work spans education, community mobilization, media engagement, and faith-based leadership.

Recognized for her resilience and compassionate leadership, Rolex holds a Master's degree from Maseno University and teaches Physics and Chemistry at Obera Boys High School.

"To help children, teens, and youth identify their inbuilt potential, amplify their voices, and become future leaders and change-makers in their communities."

Her Roles

A Woman of Many Callings

Rolex Ong'ute wears many hats — each one in service of her community.

Founder & Executive Director

Leads DESHAFO Integrated CBO from vision to implementation, ensuring sustainable impact across all programs.

Teacher of Physics & Chemistry

Serves as a dedicated educator at Obera Boys High School — modeling the academic excellence she inspires in youth.

Child Protection Advocate

A passionate public speaker and advocate for child safety, mental health, and SGBV awareness in schools and communities.

Media Panelist

Frequent guest on Ramogi TV and Radio, discussing education policy, parenting, and child development in the Lake region.

Gospel Interpreter

Serves as interpreter for Girwa Radio's weekly gospel program, bridging faith and community outreach in the local language.

Children's Minister

Leads children's ministry at MOSA Church, Homabay — recipient of the Church & Community Certificate Award for her consistency.

Her Journey

A Life of Purpose

Academic Excellence

Completed a Master's degree from Maseno University. Has been teaching Physics and Chemistry at Obera Boys High School, shaping young scientific minds.

2015 — GRETECHI Conference Begins

Launched the GRETECHI Conference in collaboration with MOSA Church — an annual event now impacting 300+ youth each August.

2019 — DESHAFO Integrated CBO Founded

Officially registered DESHAFO Integrated CBO, formalizing years of community work into an organized, mission-driven organization.

Award Recognition

Received the Church and Community Certificate Award for her unwavering consistency in community mobilization and service.

Media Engagement

Became a regular media panelist on Ramogi TV and Radio, expanding DESHAFO's reach and advocacy voice across the region.
