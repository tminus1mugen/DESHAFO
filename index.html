About DESHAFO

DEstiny SHApers FOundation

Founded in 2019 by Rolex Ong'ute, DESHAFO Integrated CBO is a registered community-based organization in Outbram Village, Central Ward, Homabay Town Sub-county.

Our mission is to create opportunities for underprivileged children, youth, and women through education, mentorship, and climate action. We also operate DESHAFO Integrated School — a testament to our commitment to accessible education.

Meet Our Founder

Rolex Ong'ute

Founder & Executive Director · Physics & Chemistry Teacher · Child Protection Advocate

A mother of three from Homabay County, Rolex holds a Master's degree from Maseno University. She teaches at Obera Boys High School and is a frequent media panelist on Ramogi TV and Radio, speaking on education, child protection, and parenting.

As Children's Minister at MOSA Church and recipient of the Church & Community Certificate Award, Rolex brings faith, resilience, and compassionate leadership to every initiative.

"To help children, teens, and youth identify their inbuilt potential, amplify their voices, and become future leaders and change-makers in their communities."
