Who We Are

A Community Built on Purpose

DESHAFO Integrated CBO is a registered non-profit Community Based Organization founded in 2019 by Rolex Ong'ute. We are based in Outbram Village, Central Ward, Homabay Town Sub-county, Homabay County, Kenya.

The name DESHAFO stands for DEstiny SHApers FOundation. "Integrated" reflects our commitment to addressing multiple dimensions of community wellbeing under one unified mission.

We serve children, youth, and women — particularly those who are underprivileged — by creating access to education, mentorship, counselling, and climate action initiatives.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To create opportunities for underprivileged children, youth, and women through education, mentorship, and climate action in Homabay County.

Our Vision

A community where every child, teen, and young person can identify their inbuilt potential, amplify their voice, and become a future leader.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, community ownership, spiritual grounding, child protection, and environmental responsibility guide everything we do.
